| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-03-17 | Information Disclosure | Pill badge reveals classification on hibernated tab list | LOW | accept | Per D-12 this is the intended UI; data never leaves the device (NFR-04) |
